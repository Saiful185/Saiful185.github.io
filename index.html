About Me

A dedicated academic and researcher at BRAC University with a passion for teaching and advancing the fields of computer science and engineering.

I am driven by a commitment to academic excellence, innovative research, and fostering a dynamic learning environment for the next generation of engineers and scientists. My work focuses on creating intelligent systems that can perceive and understand the world through multiple modalities, with a special interest in biomedical applications.
