Beyond Academia

Politics & Conviction

I've always been an outspoken and opinionated person with a deep-seated interest in socio-political issues. For me, this isn't just a passive interest; I feel a strong obligation to raise my voice against injustice. This conviction has led to some of the most surreal and meaningful experiences of my life, from participating in the movement demanding justice after Abrar Fahad's murder on our campus, to witnessing the immense courage of my own students during the July Revolution.

Sports

Sports, particularly cricket and football, are where I find one of the purest forms of happiness. I consider myself a decent cricketer and a half-decent footballer—one who knows how to be in the right place at the right time, but who also misses a lot of chances! Beyond the field, I also enjoy the strategic thinking of table tennis and chess. For me, sports are a constant source of joy and valuable lessons in discipline and teamwork.

Real Madrid C.F.

Real Madrid is the club of my life. My own competitive nature is reflected in the club's defining characteristics: the "win at any cost" mentality and, most importantly, the refusal to ever give up. The spirit of the *remontada* and the relentless pursuit of victory, even when all seems lost, is a philosophy that inspires me not just in sports, but in my own life and research.

On Page & Screen

I've always enjoyed escaping into good books, movies, and TV series, with my favorite genres being Thriller, History, and Fantasy. The magical world of Hogwarts has always intrigued me—I'm a proud Potterhead and a Gryffindor. My all-time favorite movies include the mind-bending thriller *Inception*, the heartfelt comedy *3 Idiots*, and the historical epic *Kingdom of Heaven*. When it comes to TV, I'm drawn to classics like *Sherlock*, *Game of Thrones*, *Vikings*, *Breaking Bad*, and *The Big Bang Theory*.
